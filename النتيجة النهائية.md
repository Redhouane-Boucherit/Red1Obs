## Anatomical Regions 
Main regions:
- **Head, Neck**
- **Thorax, Abdomen, Pelvis**
- **Upper extremity**
  - Shoulder
  - Arm
  - Elbow
  - Forearm
  - Wrist
  - Hand
  - Fingers
- **Lower extremity**
  - Hip
  - Thigh
  - Knee
  - Leg
  - Ankle
  - Foot
  - Toes

---

## Anatomical Position
*Reference position*:
1. Standing upright
2. Arms by the side
3. Palms facing forward
4. Thumbs away from the body
5. Toes pointing forward

---


## Directional Terms
| Term      | Meaning               |
| --------- | --------------------- |
| Superior  | Higher position       |
| Inferior  | Lower position        |
| Anterior  | Front of the body     |
| Posterior | Back of the body      |
| Medial    | Closer to the midline |
| Lateral   | Away from the midline |

---

## Movements
- **Flexion – Extension**
- **Abduction – Adduction**
- **Lateral (external) rotation – Medial (internal) rotation**
